Security

Healthcare professionals rely on ClinyPal to securely manage millions of patient records daily. Security is our top priority, and it’s not just a detail—it’s central to everything we do. Every decision we make is designed to ensure the highest level of protection for your data, providing peace of mind and trust in our service.

Your Data is Secured

Your data is protected at every level, giving you peace of mind as you use our cloud portal.

Encryption

Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using advanced encryption standards (AES-256). This ensures that your information is protected from unauthorized access and breaches. We use industry-standard protocols like TLS (Transport Layer Security) for data transmission.

Secure Communication with HTTPS

We use HTTPS with 2048-bit SSL certificates to secure communication over the internet, ensuring an additional layer of protection for all data exchanged between your browser and our servers. This guarantees that your data is safeguarded against man-in-the-middle attacks and eavesdropping. =

Data Redundancy

Our cloud infrastructure employs data redundancy across multiple geographically distributed data centers. This guarantees that your data is always available and protected against data loss. We use automated replication to ensure that your data is always up-to-date and accessible.

HIPAA Compliance

We adhere to the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) standards, ensuring that all patient data is handled with the utmost care and security required by healthcare regulations. Our systems and processes are designed to comply with all HIPAA requirements, including secure data storage and transmission.

Regular Audits

We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments to identify and mitigate potential threats. This proactive approach helps us maintain the integrity and security of our systems. Third-party penetration tests are also conducted to ensure our defenses are robust.

Access Controls

Strict access controls are in place to ensure that only authorized personnel have access to sensitive data. Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for all users to enhance security. Role-based access control (RBAC) ensures that users only have access to the data necessary for their role.

Data Backups

Automatic data backups are performed regularly to ensure that your data can be restored in case of any unforeseen incidents. Our backup strategy includes both local and off-site storage for maximum resilience. We use incremental backups to minimize storage usage and ensure quick recovery.

Secure Data Centers

We leverage industry-leading cloud services with state-of-the-art physical security measures, including 24/7 surveillance, biometric access controls, and redundant power supplies.

Compliance and Certifications

We comply with various international standards and certifications, including ISO 27001, SOC 2, and GDPR, ensuring that our data protection practices meet the highest industry standards. Regular audits and compliance checks are conducted to maintain our certifications.

Privacy Policy

Our privacy policy is transparent and outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data. We are committed to maintaining your privacy and trust. We do not sell or share your data with third parties without your consent, and we use your data only for the purposes outlined in our policy.

Incident Response

We have a comprehensive incident response plan in place to quickly address any security breaches or incidents. Our team is trained to handle incidents promptly and effectively, minimizing any potential impact on your data.

Continuous Monitoring

We continuously monitor our systems for any signs of suspicious activity or potential threats. Advanced threat detection and prevention systems are in place to provide real-time protection.

Simple Steps to Keep Your Data Safe

Enhance the security of your data and contribute to a safer digital environment. Together, we can ensure that your information remains protected and secure.

Control Who Sees What

ClinyPal's user roles ensure data security by granting specific access levels based on each user's responsibilities. This prevents unauthorized access to sensitive information.

Learn more about account permissions

Keep Your Software Updated

Ensure that your web browser, operating system, and any installed software are up to date. Updates often include critical security patches that protect against vulnerabilities. Enable automatic updates where possible.

Use Strong, Unique Passwords and MFA

Create strong, unique passwords for all your accounts, using a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters. Avoid easily guessable information. Enable multi-factor authentication (MFA) for an additional layer of security.

Learn to Secure Your Account with 2FA

Manage Browser Extensions

Only install necessary and trusted browser extensions. Review permissions requested by extensions and disable or remove any that you no longer use.

Secure Your Devices

Protect your devices with antivirus software, firewalls, and encryption. Use strong passwords or biometric authentication to lock your devices, and ensure they are secure when accessing sensitive information.

Be Cautious with Public Wi-Fi

Avoid accessing sensitive information over public Wi-Fi networks, which can be insecure. If you must use public Wi-Fi, use a Virtual Private Network (VPN) to encrypt your connection.

Monitor Account Activity

Regularly review your account activity for any suspicious or unauthorized actions. Report any suspicious activity to our support team immediately.

Found suspicious activities?

If you notice any unusual or suspicious activities in your account, please contact our support team immediately. Your security is our top priority, and our team is here to investigate and address any concerns to ensure your data remains safe.
